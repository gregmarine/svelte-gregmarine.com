# Ingredients

- 8 tbsp butter (full stick)
- 1 cup pineapple juice
- 1 cup pineapple tidbits (optional)
- 1/3 cup low sodium soy sauce
- 6 cloves garlic (minced)
- 1/2 tbsp ginger (minced)
- 1 tbsp rice vinegar
- 1/2 cup ketchup
- 1/3 cup brown sugar
- 1.5 tbsp Sriracha (add more if you want flavor heat)
